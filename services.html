Our Services

Comprehensive support for every aspect of wellbeing

Service Directory

Professional support when you need it most

Our mental health services provide confidential, compassionate support for a range of needs. All our practitioners are fully qualified and registered.

  • One-to-one counselling - 50-minute sessions with a trained counsellor
  • Listening circles - Weekly peer support groups (free)
  • Crisis support - Immediate help for urgent mental health concerns
  • Workplace wellbeing - Corporate programmes for employee mental health

Stay active, stay healthy

Physical activity is essential for overall wellbeing. Our programmes are designed for all ages and abilities.

  • Gentle yoga - Slow-paced yoga for beginners and seniors
  • Community walking groups - Social walks every Tuesday and Thursday
  • Chair-based exercises - Seated exercises for limited mobility
  • Nutrition workshops - Learn about healthy eating on a budget

Supporting families to thrive

We offer practical support and advice for families at all stages.

  • Parenting workshops - Evidence-based parenting strategies
  • Children's activities - After-school clubs and holiday programmes
  • Family mediation - Support for family conflict resolution
  • Carer support groups - For those caring for loved ones

Dignified support for older adults

Specialised services for seniors to maintain independence and quality of life.

  • Home visits - Wellbeing checks and social connection
  • Digital skills training - Help using technology to stay connected
  • Befriending service - Regular phone calls and visits
  • Benefits advice - Help accessing entitled support

How to access our services

1
Contact us by phone, email, or online form
2
Initial assessment with a wellbeing advisor
3
Referral to appropriate service or support
4
Ongoing support and review as needed

Self-referrals and professional referrals are both accepted. You can refer yourself or ask someone to refer on your behalf with your consent.
