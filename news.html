Success Story

"The walking group changed my life"

By Sarah Johnson | April 5, 2026

After losing her husband of 40 years, Sarah felt isolated and alone. "I didn't want to leave the house," she says. "A friend told me about the Wellora Hub walking group. I was nervous at first, but everyone was so welcoming." Three months later, Sarah hasn't missed a single walk. "I've made wonderful friends and I feel healthier than I have in years."
